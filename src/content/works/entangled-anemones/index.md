this text piece was created in response to the Red Sea and it’s changing ecosystems. the poem looks for ways in which the narrator is going home, or looking for filial systems in the coral structures along the coast of the sea. the threads seek to make visible the threads of logic that create the form of the poem, though the audience moves from a wandering/desperation to an illegible answer in the second poem. the viewer is invited to maneuver the text — is there an answer waiting in the second cluster? is there truly direction on the page?
