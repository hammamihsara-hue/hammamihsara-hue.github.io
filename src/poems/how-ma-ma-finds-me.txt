HOW MA-MA FINDS ME, 5 YEARS LATER
IN HONG KONG,
IMAGINED


my last dream. she speaks to me in english
the shock stirs me enough that i look up from the sidewalk
and grasp her hands
warm, smoothed, she moves to hold my hair
she laughs,          oh! it — dances — but the word starts with an s
- it snakes!, she delights

          oh, i say, confused,       not yet teary
          i would have come sooner if i had known
          yo lo puedo encontrar


i too,
look unto the night
i ask her:          ¿ cómo
nos hablamos ?

all around me:          nada de sonido


          chorus of
          low humming street lamps
          cats decrying their heat
          the last star,       winking good-
          bye
